\chapter*{Executive Summary}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Executive Summary}

Reality Anchors is a workflow intelligence system designed to reduce material waste, stabilize bench execution, and improve fabrication throughput using deterministic operational controls.

\section*{Investment Thesis}
\begin{itemize}
\item Modeled scrap reduction: 1–3 percentage points (segment dependent)
\item Linear value scaling with tonnage
\item Subscription pricing aligned to minority fraction of modeled value
\item No capital expenditure requirement
\end{itemize}

\section*{Financial Characteristics}
\begin{itemize}
\item Typical modeled annual value: \$7k–\$100k+
\item Indicative subscription capture: 10–25\%
\item Modeled ROI frequently exceeds capital equipment hurdle rates
\item Operational expense classification (not CapEx)
\end{itemize}

\chapter{Savings Calculation Model}

\section{Definitions}

\[
T = \text{Tons per month}
\]
\[
C = \text{Steel cost per ton}
\]
\[
\Delta S = \text{Scrap improvement delta}
\]

\section{Material Savings}

\[
M = 12 \times T \times C \times \Delta S
\]

\section{Labor Savings (Optional)}

\[
L = 12 \times T \times \Delta H \times W
\]

\section{Total Modeled Value}

\[
V = M + L
\]

\chapter{Subscription Alignment}

\[
P = \rho \times V
\]

Where:

\[
0.10 \leq \rho \leq 0.25
\]

\chapter{Monte Carlo Sensitivity Model}

Variables simulated:

\[
T \sim U[T_{low}, T_{high}]
\]
\[
\Delta S \sim U[\Delta S_{low}, \Delta S_{high}]
\]

\[
V = 12TC\Delta S + 12T\Delta H W
\]

\chapter{Comparative ROI}

Annual ROI:

\[
ROI = \frac{V - P}{P}
\]

Illustrative example:

\[
V = 15{,}840
\]
\[
P = 2{,}400
\]

\[
ROI = \frac{13{,}440}{2{,}400} = 5.6 = 560\%
\]

\chapter{Limitations \& Assumptions}

\begin{itemize}
\item Baselines represent median segment distributions.
\item Improvement assumes disciplined workflow adoption.
\item Steel cost volatility impacts material savings.
\item Modeled values are structured estimates, not guarantees.
\end{itemize}
